iMessage Video Preview Demo

Cosmos

This page demonstrates how to implement video previews in iMessage Rich Links. When you share this URL in iMessage, the video will autoplay inline in the message preview.

How to Test iMessage Preview:

  1. Copy the URL from the box below
  2. Open iMessage on your iPhone, iPad, or Mac
  3. Paste the URL into a conversation
  4. The video should appear and autoplay in the message preview
https://juliankellydesign.github.io/imessage-video-preview/

Note: The video will autoplay and loop with audio muted. Users can tap to unmute if audio is present.
